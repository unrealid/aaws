## Thies Clima
### Baro Transmitter/Setra (B-278-1 T) 

| Warna/No Kabel | Keterangan  | Slot Data Taker | CV    |
|----------------|:-----------:|:---------------:|:-----:|
| 5 (Vout-Green) | Analog Baro | 4HV             | 40 CV |
| 2 (AGND-White) | AGND        | 4-              | 40 CV |
| 4 (Supply-Red) | DC+12V      |                 | 40 CV |
| 3 (GND-Black)  | DC-12V      |                 | 40 CV |
